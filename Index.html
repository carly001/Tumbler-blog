Before You Buy

5 Things to Always Check

01
Leakproof vs. Splash-Resistant
NOT the same thing. YETI standard lid and Stanley H2.0 are splash-resistant only — they will leak upside-down in a bag. BrüMate, Owala, and Meoky are genuinely leakproof.
02
Lead-Free Matters
Owala, Simple Modern, Hydro Flask, and Contigo are confirmed lead-free. Stanley uses a lead-containing seal in the vacuum base. If children use the tumbler, stick to confirmed lead-free brands.
03
Hot Drinks? Check First
HydroJug Traveler is cold-only. YETI straw lids are not for hot beverages. BrüMate Era focuses on cold. For both coffee and ice water, choose Stanley, Simple Modern, Owala, or Hydro Flask.
04
Claimed Hours vs. Real Hours
Real-world retention is typically 15–25% less than brand claims. Use retention numbers as a relative ranking guide, not an absolute promise.
05
Dishwasher Safe Matters
Stanley, YETI, Simple Modern, HydroJug, Contigo, and Hydro Flask are fully dishwasher safe. Meoky and Corkcicle require hand-washing. If you won't hand-wash daily, this matters.
